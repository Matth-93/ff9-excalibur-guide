Contributing to the FFIX Excalibur II Guide
Thank you for helping improve this guide! ⚔️
Adding Screenshots
Screenshots bring the guide to life and help players know exactly what to look for.
Naming Convention
Screenshots must match their segment ID:

D1-01.png → Disc 1, Segment 01
D2-05.png → Disc 2, Segment 05
D4-12.png → Disc 4, Segment 12

Segment IDs are visible in the guide itself and in the HTML source.
How to Contribute

Fork this repository
Add your screenshot(s) to the screenshots/ folder
Open a Pull Request with a brief description
PNG or JPG accepted — original game resolution preferred

Guidelines

PlayStation or emulator screenshots both welcome
Crop to show the relevant moment clearly
No watermarks or overlays please

Questions?
Open an issue and we'll figure it out together. 🎮
